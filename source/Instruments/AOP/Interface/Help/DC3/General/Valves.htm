Valves

There are a significant number of valves required for the proper operation of the system. Incorrect positioning of the valves may result in degradation of performance (i.e. contaminated mirrors) or simple prevent sampling. Each valve should be clearly labeled such that it can be readily identified. Below is a list of valves and they're corresponding labels. Refer to this table for proper identification of the valves.
