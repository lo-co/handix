CRDS Power

Below is the list of circuit breakers as well as software switches associated with the operation of the CRDS. All power is routed through the PAS side power distribution box, so in addition to the CRDS breakers, there are several PAS side circuit breakers that must also be on. In every case, the main power must be applied and in many of the cases the DC power supplies must also be on.
